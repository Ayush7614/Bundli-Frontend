    ABOUT US

    STAND UP FOR THE WORLD is born with the purpose to make
    projects with real solutions to help our planet and protect the environment.

    Stand Up for the World is ready to make the difference through innovative eco-friendly projects,
    like: trasformation of linear economy to circular economy in order to reduce highly co2 emissions in the atmosphere,
    sustein the initiative of reforestation and protection of green zone threaten by human’s action.



    RAISING AWARENESS

    Since 2009, the primary mission of Stand up for the World Foundation has been to
    provide factual educational information to the public about environmental awareness.
    Stand up for the World Foundation has granted research funding to
    numerous colleges and universities, building various ecological centers at some of the
    most prominent institutions of higher learning.



    INITIATIVES

    As a non-profit public benefit corporation, Stand up for the World Foundation strives
    to help make the changes necessary to provide a healthier future for our planet
    by funding our Global Initiatives. We aim to fund the single most impacting environmental changes
    worldwide in an effort to slow and ultimately reverse the deterioration of our planet earth.
